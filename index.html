Pastor Elizabeth Wetzler

Pastor Elizabeth has a background in accounting who studing business adminstration at George Washington University. She was born in Bogota, Colombia and has lived in Both Longview, Tx and Rockville, MD. Having led English and Spanish spoken ministries in three different churches, her vision is bring togeth multie cultures for one purpose and that is to share and equip all ethnities of the good news of Jesus Christ. She has led several ministries, from Praise and Worship to local and overseas missions trips. In 2011 Pastor Elizabeth began her pastoral studies at Latin American Bible Institute later graduating in 2015. God gave her vision to serve Gaithersburg and it's surroung communities to give birth to a life giving, christ-centered, disciple making church.

Martin Wetzler

Martin Wetzler has a background in construction safety and health and brings a nurturing spirit to the church body. He was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina though lived most of his life in the metropolitan are of Rockville, MD. He has led sound and technology ministies and co-led a few local and overseas mission trips. He loves endurance sports and competes in triathlons and long distance running.
